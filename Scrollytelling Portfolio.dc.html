05 — FEATURED CASE STUDY

Veycho Resto-Café

A full-stack restaurant & hospitality platform — designed, built and deployed to production at veycho.in, now serving a real café in Wayanad, Kerala.

Next.js 16TypeScriptSupabaseVercelLive in production
Visit veycho.in →
THE PROBLEM

A growing café needed more than a social page — a fast, branded web presence where guests could browse the menu, explore the area and get in touch.

THE SOLUTION

A production Next.js 16 site on Vercel with a custom domain, backed by Supabase for real-time menu, gallery and content management.

ARCHITECTURE

Next.js App Router → Supabase (Postgres + Storage) → Vercel edge

KEY FEATURES

3-phase animated page transitions · dynamic menu explorer · masonry photo gallery with lightbox · AI concierge chatbot · WhatsApp & Instagram FABs.
